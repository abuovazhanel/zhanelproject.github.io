To book a flight ticket, please select: flight type (one-way, round-trip, or multi-city); city of departure and arrival; date of travel; number of passengers. Click on "Search".

During booking choose the age of child: 0–2 years old. With one adult, it is allowed to book only one infant ticket (without an extra seat). Infants may travel only accompanied by adults. Please note that infants are not provided with a separate seat. If you are flying with two or more infants, please check the booking details with Customer Care Service or the airline. If you would like to order a cabin crib for the baby, let our Customer Care Service know of it. We will contact the airline and let you know whether it is possible.

During booking, choose age of child: 2-12 years old. Children's flight tickets include a separate seat and provide for a certain baggage allowance (which is usually lower than the one for the standard fare).
